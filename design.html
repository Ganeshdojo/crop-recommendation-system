How AgroPredicta Works

Our AI-powered platform simplifies crop prediction while delivering powerful insights for your farm.

Step 1

Input Parameters

Enter soil composition (NPK values), environmental factors (temperature, humidity, rainfall), and pH level. For convenience, you can use geolocation to automatically fetch weather data.

Step 2

Model Selection

Choose from multiple machine learning models like Random Forest and XGBoost, or train your own custom model with specific datasets for your region and soil type.

Step 3

Process Data

Our AI algorithms analyze your inputs against historical data and current conditions to make intelligent predictions based on complex patterns and relationships.

Step 4

Get Predictions

Receive accurate crop recommendations along with confidence scores, growing guidelines, and soil improvement suggestions tailored to your specific conditions.
